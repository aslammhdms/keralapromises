## What was promised

The UDF 2026 manifesto commits to setting up a Tribal University in Wayanad. The promise is structural: a new university, not a study centre or scheme within an existing institution.

## What is unclear from the manifesto text alone

- Whether the university will be constituted by a fresh Act of the Kerala Legislative Assembly or as a constituent of an existing university.
- The medium of instruction and the position on tribal-language pedagogy.
- The land, capital outlay, and timeline for first admissions.
- The academic remit: tribal studies and language, or a fuller curriculum with reserved seats and outreach.

We will record these as they appear in a bill, a government order, or budget documents.

## How we will assess fulfilment

The promise reaches `in_progress` when a cabinet decision or government order constituting the university is announced, or when a bill is introduced in the Assembly. It reaches `fulfilled` when the university is constituted by Act, has begun admitting students, and operates with substantive autonomy as specified in its founding statute.

Renaming or relabelling of an existing institution will not count as fulfilment.
