## What was promised

The UDF 2026 manifesto commits to a monthly allowance of ₹1,000 for female college students in Kerala, announced as one of the five "Indira guarantees" at the manifesto launch on 2 April 2026.

## What is unclear from the manifesto text alone

- Whether the allowance applies to all enrolled students at government, aided, and self-financing institutions, or only to specific categories.
- The eligibility floor: family income limits, attendance criteria, course type (UG, PG, professional).
- Mode of disbursement: bank transfer, scholarship portal, college-mediated.

We will record these as they appear in government orders.

## How we will assess fulfilment

The promise reaches `in_progress` when a cabinet decision or government order constituting the scheme is announced. It reaches `fulfilled` when the order is operational and eligible students are receiving the allowance, on the terms the operational order specifies.

A pilot in a limited number of districts will be recorded as `in_progress`, not `fulfilled`.
