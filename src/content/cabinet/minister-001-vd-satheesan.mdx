## Verified entry

V. D. Satheesan took oath as Chief Minister of Kerala on 18 May 2026 at the Central Stadium, Thiruvananthapuram. The oath was administered by Governor Rajendra Vishwanath Arlekar. He leads the UDF government formed after the alliance won 102 of 140 Assembly seats.

## Portfolio status

On 20 May 2026, the portfolio allocation was announced and submitted to Governor Arlekar; Onmanorama reports it as published in an Extraordinary Gazette notification. The Chief Minister retains **Finance**, **Law**, **Ports**, **Planning**, **General Administration**, and a wider cluster of departments.
