## What was promised

The UDF 2026 manifesto commits to setting up mobile chemotherapy units in Kerala, intended to bring oncology day-care services closer to patients who currently travel long distances to tertiary centres.

## What is unclear from the manifesto text alone

- The number of units to be deployed and their geographic catchment.
- The operating agency: Regional Cancer Centre, Malabar Cancer Centre, district hospitals, or a new vertical.
- The patient referral pathway and which regimens are administered on a mobile basis.

We will record these as they appear in government orders.

## How we will assess fulfilment

The promise reaches `in_progress` when a cabinet decision, government order, or tender for the units is announced. It reaches `fulfilled` when units are operational and treating patients, on the terms the operational order specifies.
