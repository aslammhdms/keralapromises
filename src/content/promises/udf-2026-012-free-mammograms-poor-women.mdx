## What was promised

The UDF 2026 manifesto commits to making mammograms free for women from poor families, as part of the broader health and women-and-child set of commitments.

## What is unclear from the manifesto text alone

- The income or eligibility floor defining "poor families".
- Whether the service is delivered at government facilities only, or through a public-private referral channel.
- The age band and screening frequency.
- Whether breast-cancer follow-up (biopsy, treatment) is included or remains under existing schemes.

We will record these as they appear in government orders.

## How we will assess fulfilment

The promise reaches `in_progress` when a cabinet decision, government order, or tender for the service is announced. It reaches `fulfilled` when free mammograms are available to eligible women at scale across government health facilities, on the terms the operational order specifies.
