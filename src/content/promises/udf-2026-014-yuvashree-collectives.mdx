## What was promised

The UDF 2026 manifesto commits to introducing Yuvashree collectives modelled on Kudumbashree, Kerala's well-established women's self-help and livelihood mission.

## What is unclear from the manifesto text alone

- Whether membership is limited to young women, or open to youth of all genders.
- The age band defining "yuva".
- The institutional anchor: the Local Self-Government department, a new mission, or an extension of Kudumbashree.
- The financial model: revolving funds, state grants, market-linked credit.

We will record these as they appear in government orders.

## How we will assess fulfilment

The promise reaches `in_progress` when a cabinet decision or government order constituting the mission is announced. It reaches `fulfilled` when collectives are operating at scale across panchayats with documented membership and a budget head, on the terms the operational order specifies.

A pilot in a limited number of districts will be recorded as `in_progress`, not `fulfilled`.
